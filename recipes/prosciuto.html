Prosciutto e Melone (Italian Ham and Melon)

Description

Easy to make and incredibly delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 cantaloupe - seeded and cut into 8 wedges
  • 8 thin slices prosciutto

Steps

  1. Step 1: Remove the flesh from the rind of the cantaloupe; wrap each piece of cantaloupe with a slice of prosciutto. Arrange on a platter and serve cold.
