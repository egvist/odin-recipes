Baked Tomato Slices

Description

Baked Tomato Slices recipe is here for you. It takes only 15 minutes and it is ready to serve!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided, or as needed
  • 1 large tomato, cut into 1/2-inch-thick slices
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, leaves stripped and finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Brush a baking sheet with about 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  2. Step 2: Arrange tomato slices on the baking sheet; sprinkle rosemary and garlic on top. Brush with remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil; season with salt and pepper.
  3. Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven until tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
